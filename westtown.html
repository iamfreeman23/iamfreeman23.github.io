Boürdain West Town – the place where great hair days and effortless style begins.

At the Boürdain West Town Chicago hair salon we want you to relax and experience a hair salon like no other. Our amazing hairstylists focus on fulfilling every hair service you need. We’ve created a serene environment where we do our best to make you feel beautiful each and every day. Our professional staff will deliver a personalized service to exceed your expectations upon each and every visit.

Making a Difference One Haircut at a Time

The Boürdain West Town hair salon is the winner of Salon Today’s 2017 and 2011 ”Salon of Distinction Award!” This charming beauty salon is the newest location to join the Boürdain Salon family, incorporating rustic and vintage modern elements influenced by the culture and ambiance of the West Town and Ukrainian Village neighborhoods. This chic urban haven opened in 2011 and has been a roaring success in the Chicago hairdressing scene ever since.

Instagram-worthy hair is just a visit away

From new color techniques like Balayage hair painting to inventive haircuts, to mens hair design and barbering– the best hair stylists in Chicago have been trained here. We are passionate and consumed by all things hair and health, and have created by hand, our own brand of clean conscious, nourishing all natural products that are inspired by our healthy lifestyle. We offer complimentary fruit-infused water, coffee, tea, wine and PBR for new and returning customers as they relax and enjoy the best hair services in Chicago’s West Town. Boürdain also offers Boürdain Points where clients can receive point credits for future services by pre-booking, and referring friends. Book an appointment with one of our fabulous stylists today and start enjoying the perks of having the perfect hairstyle that’s effortlessly right for you.
