Our Boürdain Lakeview location is one of the most visited hair salons in Chicago. Boürdain Lakeview – a top destination for heavenly hair styles and modern hair coloring techniques

We focus on creating beautiful work with impeccable customer service for a unique and outstanding client experience each and every time!

As masters of our craft, Boürdain Lakeview is leading the new Renaissance for fashion and hair in the exploding Lincoln and Roscoe neighborhood. Come and visit us for every hair-color trend of the moment like balayage highlights, women and men’s haircuts, a blowout bar, braids and formal styling and bridal hair services including updos for your special day! this elite Chicago hair salon offers brow shaping, waxing, lash and brow tinting and more to create flattering looks for our guests. Our philosophy is to deliver superior, personalized hair styles that look lived in, in the best possible way.
