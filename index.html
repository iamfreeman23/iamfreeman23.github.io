BOÜRDAIN Salon: Open 26 years, consistently voted best Hair Salon in Chicago.

At Boürdain we have built a reputation for customized, caring service performed by expert innovative hair stylists in our three salons in Chicago IL. Frequently named the best Chicago Hair Salon, Boürdain curates on-trend hair styles, specializing in women’s haircuts, men’s haircuts and barbering, highlighting, haircolor, bridal hair, blowouts, and the latest color techniques, including balayage, ombre and creative hair painting.
Our focus on advanced hair stylist training ensures that we do the highest quality of work by delivering an extensive advanced education program for young stylists in our dynamic environment. Open since 1993, Boürdain sets the standard in the beauty industry by offering a flexible, creative, and supportive environment for the best hair stylists in Chicago to thrive in.
