ABOUT

STYLING CHICAGO SINCE 1993

THE HOME OF THE BEST HAIR STYLISTS IN CHICAGO. ARTISTIC, CUSTOMIZED HAIR CUTS AND COLOR THAT GIVE YOU THAT EFFORTLESSLY PERFECT LOOK.

BOÜRDAIN: AN INDISPENSABLE OR ESSENTIAL INGREDIENT – “That which you cant live without”

Boürdain Salons was founded in 1993 by native Chicagoan, Laura Boton. Celebrating 26 years in business, Boürdain’s award-winning salons in Chicago deliver excellent artistic hair services in a friendly and restorative environment. Considering Laura’s background as a rock musician and vocal major at De Paul, it’s not surprising that BOÜRDAIN quickly rose to fame on the local arts scene, attracting Chicago’s creative community to call Boürdain their go-to hair salon. Our team of inventive hair stylists create flattering, on trend easy to wear looks that fit each individual perfectly.

Consistently awarded Best Hair Salon in Chicago – our three locations are the place to experience superb, on-trend service. We are the Boürdain of hair salons in Chicago.

“Of all the beauty salons in Chicago, Boürdain tops the list”. -Time Out Chicago

At Boürdain, our stylists design your cut and color to create the “Total Look”. One visionary stylist creates the perfect cut and color for each guest, delivering the very best in one-on-one service.

Known for our technical and forward thinking, non-departmentalized stylist advanced training program, Boürdain Salons commitment to education and growth are second to none. All stylists are trained to be the very best at their craft and are committed to being in touch with every new trend and technique, keeping us fresh and inspired.

Honored with industry accolades and awards by major fashion magazines for hair styling, coloring, brow shaping and curly hair specialization since 1993– Boürdain has developed a loyal following of guests who seek customization, attention to detail, and talent like no other.

Come in. Get comfortable. Get styled!
